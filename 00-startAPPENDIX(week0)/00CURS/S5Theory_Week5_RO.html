IPv4 to IPv6 Transition Mechanisms

The Internet cannot switch overnight. Coexistence strategies enable gradual migration.

Dual-Stack

Devices run both IPv4 and IPv6 simultaneously. Applications choose based on destination availability.

Tunelare

IPv6 packets encapsulated inside IPv4 for transport across IPv4-only networks. Examples: 6to4, Teredo.

Translation (NAT64)

Translates between IPv6 and IPv4 at network boundaries, allowing IPv6-only clients to reach IPv4 servers.
